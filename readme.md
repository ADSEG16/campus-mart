# CampusMart

CampusMart is a student-to-student marketplace built for campus communities.

It was created to move campus buying and selling away from unstructured social media chats into a safer, more reliable, and easier-to-use platform.

---
## Purpose
Students often trade through informal group chats where scams, confusion, and poor visibility are common. CampusMart aims to solve that by offering:

1. A trusted environment for campus trading
2. Better visibility for listings and sellers
3. A clear process from interest to completed exchange
4. Community safety through moderation and accountability

## Target Users
1. Students who want to buy and sell items on campus
2. Campus admins or moderators keeping the platform safe

## Key Features
1. Student account onboarding and verification
2. Listing discovery across categories
3. In-app messaging between buyers and sellers
4. Transaction flow designed for campus meetups
5. Notifications and activity updates
6. Admin tools for reports, moderation, and oversight

---
## Why It Matters

CampusMart supports a safer campus economy by encouraging responsible trading habits, reducing anonymous fraud, and creating a stronger sense of trust in student-to-student exchanges.
